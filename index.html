Rationale

I've always believed that most government data should be transparent and open to all. In the case of PSA, they do have quite a lot of databases and resources available to the general public, it's just that not everyone is techy enough to navigate their current website, which is full of articles andd unresponsive links. I wanted to give their site a modern feel, and put live stats on the landing page. I was more successful doing the former.

This redesign isn't meant to replace the current PSA website, but rather take a few of it's features away from the main site. Namely, its downloadables -- databases, their publications, and annual reports. I believe that if implemented, many more people that are curious about Philippine statistics and data will have an easier time looking for these records, and will save them a lot of time and effort.
